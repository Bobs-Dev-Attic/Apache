Apache — Controls

Flight (Keyboard)

  • W / S — Tilt forward / back
  • A / D — Bank left / right
  • Space — Power (fly in tilt direction)
  • Q / E — Yaw (pedals) left / right
  • Shift — Collective up (climb)
  • Ctrl — Collective down (descend)

Camera

  • Left-drag — Rotate isometric view
  • Right-drag — Pan
  • Wheel — Zoom in / out
  • Touch: 1 finger rotate, 2 finger pinch-zoom & pan
  • F — Follow helicopter on/off

On mobile: left thumb-stick tilts, hold PWR to fly in that direction; right buttons control collective & yaw.
