Apache — Controls

Flight (Keyboard)

  • W / S — Tilt forward / back (powers forward / back)
  • A / D — Bank left / right (powers sideways)
  • Q / E — Yaw (pedals) left / right
  • C / Shift — Collective up (climb)
  • Z / Ctrl — Collective down (descend)
  • G — Arm main gun; right-click to fire at cursor
  • R — Rocket sensor mode; Tab cycles targets
  • M — Missile (guided) sensor mode; Tab cycles
  • 1 / 2 — Fire rockets / missiles at locked target
  • F — Deploy flares (countermeasures)

Camera

  • Left-drag — Rotate isometric view
  • Right-drag — Pan
  • Wheel — Zoom in / out
  • Touch: 1 finger rotate, 2 finger pinch-zoom & pan
  • V — Follow helicopter on/off
  • 🎥 button — Cycle camera views (chase, rear, side, top-down…)

On mobile: the left stick is the cyclic — tilt it to fly in that direction (the further you push, the more power); the right stick climbs/descends (▲/▼) and yaws (◀/▶). The 2×2 pad above the left stick selects a weapon (GUN / RKT / MSL, ✦ flares); the FIRE button shoots — hold for the gun, tap for rockets/missiles. Tap the sensor screen (under the radar) to cycle targets. Weapons and fuel are limited — watch the gauges; open the ☰ menu for a New Game.
