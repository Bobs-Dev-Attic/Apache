Apache — Controls

Flight (Keyboard)

  • W / S — Tilt forward / back
  • A / D — Bank left / right
  • Space — Power (fly in tilt direction)
  • Q / E — Yaw (pedals) left / right
  • C / Shift — Collective up (climb)
  • Z / Ctrl — Collective down (descend)
  • G — Arm main gun; right-click to fire at cursor
  • R — Rocket sensor mode; Tab cycles targets
  • M — Missile (guided) sensor mode; Tab cycles
  • 1 / 2 — Fire rockets / missiles at locked target
  • F — Deploy flares (countermeasures)

Camera

  • Left-drag — Rotate isometric view
  • Right-drag — Pan
  • Wheel — Zoom in / out
  • Touch: 1 finger rotate, 2 finger pinch-zoom & pan
  • V — Follow helicopter on/off

On mobile: left thumb-stick tilts, hold PWR to fly in that direction; right buttons control collective & yaw. Left-edge buttons select a weapon (GUN / RKT / MSL, ⟳ cycles targets); the right-edge FIRE button shoots (hold for the gun, tap for rockets/missiles) and ✦ drops flares.
